Recipe #1: Chicken Alfredo Pasta

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 2 pieces
Fettucine pasta 300 grams
Heavy cream 1 cup
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup
Butter 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper to taste

Steps for preparing:

Step 1: Cook the fettucine pasta according to package instructions, then drain.
Step 2: In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
Step 3: Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and fully cooked. Slice into strips.
Step 4: Add heavy cream to the pan and bring to a simmer. Stir in Parmesan cheese until smooth.
Step 5: Add the cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce, toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper.
Step 6: Serve with extra Parmesan cheese and enjoy!

Source: Food Network Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Recipe #2: Vegetable Stir-Fry

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Bell peppers (red, yellow, green) 2 pieces
Carrot 1 medium
Broccoli florets 1 cup
Snow peas 1/2 cup
Soy sauce 3 tablespoons
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Ginger (grated) 1 teaspoon
Olive oil 2 tablespoons

Steps for preparing:

Step 1: Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
Step 2: Add the garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
Step 3: Add the carrots, bell peppers, and broccoli. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
Step 4: Add the soy sauce and snow peas. Stir well to coat the vegetables in the sauce.
Step 5: Cook for another 2 minutes, then serve with rice or noodles.

Source: AllRecipes Vegetable Stir-Fry

Recipe #3: Chocolate Lava Cake

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Dark chocolate 100 grams
Butter 1/2 cup
Eggs 2 large
Flour 1/4 cup
Powdered sugar 1/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon

Steps for preparing:

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and grease two ramekins.
Step 2: Melt the chocolate and butter in a bowl over simmering water, stirring until smooth.
Step 3: In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract.
Step 4: Fold in the melted chocolate mixture and flour.
Step 5: Pour the batter into the ramekins and bake for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are firm but the center is soft.
Step 6: Let cool for 1-2 minutes before inverting onto plates. Serve warm with ice cream or berries.

Source: Taste of Home Lava Cake Recipe
